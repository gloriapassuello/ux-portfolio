My Story

I come from Italy but I am currently based in Munich, Germany. In the past I have also lived in France, Belgium and Scotland and as a result I enjoy working in multilingual contexts and with individuals from different backgrounds. I discovered UX design while working as a recruiter in a tech company. This may seem like a drastic change but in some ways, the two fields are more similar than you might think. For example, human resources is all about empathy, listening, observation, and being mindful of the candidateâ€™s journey throughout the hiring process.

Additionally, as a recruiter, I always sit in between the business requirements and the candidate, trying to liaise with both parties to reach the best solution. Sounds familiar? All of this made my transition to UX easy and thanks to my interviewing and listening skills I am extremely adept at user research and the translation of research findings into meaningful design solutions. I would however consider myself as a generalist as I like to dive into new fields and pick up new skills quickly. For example, I am currently working on my coding skills and this is my first self-coded portfolio website. Outside of design, you can find me brushing up on my French in language events, planning my next trip, going on a hunt for the best wine shops in town or trying to perform some acrobatics in a trampolines fun park.
